MAREoS benchmark.

Tool to assess the efficacy of Methods Aimed to Remove the Effects of the Site (MAREoS).

Public datasets section.

This section provides 10 public datasets to measure the effects of the site (EoS)-removal efficacy of a MAREoS.
A step-by-step tutorial is provided.

Upload section.

This section provides 10 benchmark datasets. Developers can download them, remove the effects of the site using a MAREoS, and upload the resulting datasets to add the MAREoS in the benchmark section.

Benchmark section.

This section provides a ranking of the EoS-removal efficacy of different MAREoS. For now, only ComBat is listed.



This tool is based on the calculations described in Solanes, Gosling et al. (2022).
Please, contact Joaquim Radua for more information regarding this tool's approach.
